an introduction

my name is imogen, but most people call me immie!

having a passion in both playing and creating games, i've been expanding my skills in both as i've grown up. which has lead me down the path of development for games, software and websites (like this one!).

outside of gaming, i love learning new skills and being a creative. other hobbies of mine like video editing, music, food, and languages are all things that i like to explore and develop in my free time.
i like to combine them together in unique ways, such as creating audio-visual art, or learning to cook foods from different countries and cultures.

a few short facts about me: my favourite game is sound voltex! i am an active learner of japanese and my favourite food is nduja pasta!
